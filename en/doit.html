Do It

Workout App

Overview

Do It is a sports management app. This project is a concept from desgin training course. The original idea come from one of team members who has workout regularly in life. because the online app cannot meet the requirements, so we decide to create a App.

My Role

App icon design, Presentation

Timeline

2015-2016

Team members

廖秀軒, 楊雅慧, 陳麗玲, 陳姿伃

Positioning

FULL-BODY WORKOUT

The function of self-health management, avoiding setting the time during exercise repeatedly, It can effectively plan a week of action and achieve your ideal body.


Target Audience

The app is not for beginner and didn't have tutorial about exercise, so the user have to understand how to do standard action. It is suitable for fitness instructors or users who usually workout by self.

App Icon Design


Design concept

Dumbbells represent fitness and combine dumbbells with hourglasses to metaphorize the timing function in the app.

Designing process

When I designed the App Icon, my first idea was combine the function of timing and exercise. The first version is on the left one, when I was combine the hourglass and the dumbbell and show to team member confidently, they just misunderstood as "diabolo". Although I felt upset, I still changed my design again and again until I finished final design, everyone was accepted and liked it. This is a process that made me feel very interesting. When I was design by myself, it is easy to have a blind spot. By teamwork and discussion, the final result was better than I imagined.

Color


Color selection

I choose to bring vibrant colors, like the concept we provided by the app, sporty and energetic.
